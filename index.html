I am a designer based in Sofia, Bulgaria, with a degree from the University of Sheffield and a career that has taken me across Germany and the United States. I currently lead product design at Living Homes Dubai, where the focus is on IoT, smart living solutions, and AI-powered 3D virtual assistants.

Previously, I led the design team at Visteon – Sofia as Design Manager, working on advanced HMI, UI/UX, motion graphics, and real-time 2D/3D design for the automotive industry. With over a decade of experience, I've contributed to projects across various sectors and platforms.

My work has been featured at major events including CES, Gitex Global, and Shanghai Auto.
