about.

I design products and experiences for companies building the next generation of technology - pushing the boundaries of what technology can feel like. From Daimler's award-winning MBUX to an AI-native home that thinks, learns, and speaks. My work has been featured at CES, GITEX, and Shanghai Auto.

I hold a degree from the University of Sheffield, and my career has taken me across Germany and the U.S. Currently, I'm the Lead Product Designer at Living Homes Dubai, where we focus on IoT, smart living solutions and AI-powered 3D virtual assistants. Previously, I led the design team at Visteon - Sofia as Design Manager, working on advanced HMI, UI/UX, motion graphics, and real-time 2D/3D design for the automotive industry. With over a decade of experience, I've contributed to projects across various sectors and platforms. My work has been featured at major events like CES, Gitex Global, and Shanghai Auto, and I've collaborated with clients including General Motors, Ford, Daimler, TEDx, Cannes Lions, Living Homes, and Maserati.
