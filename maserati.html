Maserati

As a huge fan of the brand, I was very honoured to be part of the design team responsible for the creation of the User Experience for the new generation Maserati vehicles. Our task was to craft the UX for the Infotainment screen — aligning with the latest design trends while accommodating a large number of applications. The project spanned 24 months, from concept to production across three vehicle models including the Maserati Grecale. Due to NDA, I am not able to show all details.
