I collaborate with brands and studios across automotive, IoT, and AI — using the latest technologies. My designs have been featured at CES, GITEX, and Shanghai Auto.

With over a decade of experience, I've contributed to projects across various sectors and platforms. My work has been featured at major events like CES, Gitex Global, and Shanghai Auto, and I've collaborated with clients including General Motors, Ford, Daimler, TEDx, Cannes Lions, Living Homes, and Maserati.
