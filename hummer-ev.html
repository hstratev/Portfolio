For this project we have partnered with the New York based design firm Perception and General Motors to create the complete user experience and interface for the First all electric super truck - Hummer EV. Target Groups & UX Challenge

Designing the user experience (UX) for the Hummer EV posed a unique challenge: marrying a sci-fi, futuristic feel with addressing the specific concerns of General Motors' existing user base. This required a delicate balance between catering to the expectations of two distinct groups.

Firstly, there were the new Hummer EV users and buyers, who were seeking a futuristic, almost sci-fi-like driving experience. For this group, the focus was on infusing the UX with elements of luxury and excitement, transforming the act of driving into a captivating, high-tech adventure.

On the flip side, there were the loyal, existing Hummer users, typically from an older generation, who cherished the rugged, military-like aura of the traditional Hummer. These users held a preference for the robust off-road capabilities that defined the Hummer brand.

In essence, the task was to create a seamless UX that bridged the gap between these two disparate user groups, incorporating futuristic, sci-fi elements for the new enthusiasts while retaining the rugged, off-road appeal for the traditionalists.
