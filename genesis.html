Genesis

Genesis is a Generation 3 AI-native home intelligence platform designed for premium residential living. As the founding designer and later Product Design Lead, I built the entire product experience from scratch - UI screens, interaction and real time animations, 3D avatar flow, and design system. The platform centers around Teya, a human-like AI home steward supported by a team of specialized AI Expert agents covering Sleep, Health, Stress, Physio, Home Utility, Entertainment, and Kids Learning - all working together to make the home proactively care for its residents.
