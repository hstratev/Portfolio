Frontier

Frontier is a versatile multi-device App designed to streamline the installation, removal, and personalisation of software applications across various platforms. It guarantees a secure and consistent user experience while automating these processes. Due to NDA, I am not able to show all details related to the project.

Project Lead — UI/UX
Internal
2023
Software / App Store

Overview

This project facilitates the uploading and deployment of applications, offering a secure app verification process. Users can customize their brand presence and segment distinctive features or applications. The system supports validation and certification of apps, and provides exclusive content delivering a smooth and uninterrupted user experience. Integration with system-wide wallets enhances convenience for transactions and purchases.

Users and Audience

The project was divided into three sections: an app developers console and portal, a customer (companies) app management and distribution portal, and an app store interface for the end user. Each section required its own distinct UX approach while maintaining a consistent visual language across the platform.

Design Process

The process encompassed collaboration among diverse teams across four regions, including user interviews within popular app store communities. This involved crafting initial prototypes, gathering feedback, and refining designs. We established a comprehensive design system that includes color palettes, buttons, filter dropdowns, navigation menus, form elements, icons, and more. The platform supports both Light and Dark themes.

Motion and Interaction

Conclusion

The project achieved success with its first significant customer secured, and was presented at the Consumer Electronics Show. The close collaboration with software developers and stakeholders across all phases of development ensured that the final product met both technical and user-facing requirements.
