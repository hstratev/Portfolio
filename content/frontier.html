Frontier

Frontier is a versatile multi-device App designed to streamline the installation, removal, and personalisation of software applications across various platforms. It guarantees a secure and consistent user experience while automating these processes.

Overview

This project facilitates the uploading and deployment of applications, offering a secure app verification process. Users can customize their brand presence and segment distinctive features or applications. The system supports the validation and certification of apps, ensuring their quality.

The platform provides exclusive content, delivering a smooth and uninterrupted user experience. Additionally, it offers an optional company based solution, aligning with specific brand requirements. Integration with system-wide wallets enhances convenience for transactions and purchases.

Problem Statement

The project aimed to build a scalable cloud-based App Store with: Scalable cloud infrastructure, not on-premise, storing data on Frontier or customer servers and handling all maintenance. Constant addition of 3rd party content providers for a customized content model and system-wide wallet integration.

Frontier's App Store to allow customers to manage their app content and offer tailored features. A versatile private backoffice for client companies to distribute apps with advanced filters. A dedicated validation and certification process in a trusted environment.

Users and Audience

The project was divided into three sections of equal importance. It consists of App developers console and portal, Customer (companies) app management & distribution portal and App Store interface for the end user.

App Developers Console and Portal: Potential users of this section are software developers, programmers, and tech enthusiasts who are involved in creating applications for various purposes. These individuals use the console and portal to manage, develop, test, and deploy their apps.

Customer (Companies) App Management & Distribution Portal: This section caters to businesses and enterprises that offer applications to their customers or employees. Users here include managers, administrators, or technical staff within these companies. They use the portal to oversee app distribution, manage permissions, customize features, and ensure that the right apps are available to the right people within their organization.

App Store Interface for End Users: The end users are the general public or individuals who access the applications through the App Store. These users could be anyone with a smartphone, tablet, or other compatible devices seeking to discover, download, and utilize various apps for personal or professional use.

This system includes a range of components such as color palettes, buttons, filter dropdowns, navigation menus, form elements, icons, and more.
